Instructions

  1. Make sure your Anzy app is installed on your device
  2. Click the universal links (https://theanzy.com/...) - these should open in your app
  3. If universal links don't work, try the custom scheme links (anzy://...)
  4. Universal links require the app to be properly configured and the device to trust the domain

Debugging

If universal links aren't working:

  • Check that your app is properly built with the correct bundle identifier
  • Verify that the universal link files are accessible at the correct URLs
  • For iOS: Check Settings > General > About > Apps Using Universal Links
  • For Android: Check if the app appears in the app chooser when clicking links
